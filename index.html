Blink and you'll miss it. Scan and you'll get it.

StoryFrame transforms live moments into instant understanding. Branded QR touchpoints placed around a venue allow spectators to scan and unlock fast, mobile-friendly micro-stories explaining the athletes, rules, rivalries and stakes behind the action with no app required. Content is delivered through a lightweight microsite and CMS, enabling real-time updates and seamless sponsor integration.

Every interaction is measurable. StoryFrame provides scan data, location insights and engagement analytics, creating new digital inventory and commercial opportunities for venues and rights-holders. It's scalable, easy to deploy and designed to enhance the live experience without adding operational complexity.
